Privacy Policy for BackgroundMute
Last updated: 2026-06-12

BackgroundMute is a Windows desktop application that mutes selected applications when they are in the background and unmutes them when they are active.
The app is designed to process information locally on your device.

BackgroundMute does not collect, store, or transmit personal data to the developer.
BackgroundMute does not include built-in analytics, advertising trackers, or telemetry services.

To function, BackgroundMute may access running process information for apps you choose to control, foreground or minimized window state, per-application audio session state, and local app settings such as selected apps and preferences.
This information is used only to provide mute and unmute functionality.

BackgroundMute does not sell or share your personal data.
Data used by the app remains on your device unless you choose to share information yourself, such as when submitting a bug report.

BackgroundMute may be distributed through third-party platforms, including GitHub Releases and WinGet.
Those platforms may collect data under their own privacy policies.
Please review the privacy terms of any platform you use to download or install the app.

Because app behavior and settings are local, your data security also depends on your device security and operating system protections.

BackgroundMute is a general-purpose utility and is not directed to children.

This policy may be updated over time.
The latest version will be available in this repository.

For questions about this privacy policy, open an issue in this repository:
https://github.com/Myst1024/BackgroundMute/issues
